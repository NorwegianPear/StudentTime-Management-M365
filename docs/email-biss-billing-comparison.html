How Azure App Service Is Billed

Azure App Service is billed per hour and invoiced monthly. There is no built-in quarterly or annual billing period in Azure — your invoice arrives once a month. However, Microsoft offers Reserved Instances for certain tiers that let you commit to 1 or 3 years upfront at a significant discount.

⚠️ Important: I checked your subscription's RateCard and the public Reservations catalogue for Norway East. The Basic tier (B1) does NOT support reserved instances — it is Pay-As-You-Go only. Reservations are only available from Premium v3 / v4 and Isolated v2 tiers upward.

💡 Save During School Holidays

Because PAYG charges stop the moment you delete or stop the App Service Plan, you can reduce costs during long breaks:

PeriodDurationSavingRunning total/year
Run all year (worst case) 12 months — 1 572 NOK
Pause July + Aug (summer) 10 months −262 NOK 1 310 NOK
Pause Jul, Aug + Christmas (2 wks) ~9.5 months −327 NOK 1 245 NOK

Pausing = deleting the App Service Plan (takes ~2 min). Redeploy takes ~5 min. Automation runbooks are unaffected — they continue running independently.
