🔎 Detailed Explanation — Why Each Entity Fails

❌ BISS Sentrum AS & BISS Preschool AS — Aksjeselskap (Limited Company)

An Aksjeselskap (AS) is a Norwegian limited liability company — the same corporate form as any commercial business. It has private shareholders, distributes profits as dividends, and exists to generate return on investment. This is the most fundamental disqualifier. Microsoft's nonprofit program is explicitly unavailable to for-profit entities regardless of sector or mission.

Capital confirmed in registry: BISS Sentrum AS has 100 shares at 1,000 NOK each (kr 100,000 share capital). BISS Preschool AS has 1,000 shares at 100 NOK each (kr 100,000 share capital). Both are registered in the commercial company registry (Foretaksregisteret).
⚠️ BISS Gausel STI — Stiftelse, but commercial (Sector 2100)

BISS Gausel is registered as a Stiftelse (Foundation), which in Norway can be either charitable (ideell stiftelse) or commercial (næringsdrivende stiftelse). The key differentiators are:

  • Institutional sector code 2100 ("Private aksjeselskaper mv.") — This is the same classification as commercial companies, NOT the charitable/NGO sector (7000–8000). This is assigned by Statistics Norway and indicates how the entity economically operates.
  • NOT registered in Frivillighetsregisteret — Norway's Voluntary Organizations Registry (run by Brønnøysundregistrene) is where genuine charities and nonprofits register to access public grants and charitable status. BISS Gausel is absent from this registry.
  • Registered in Stiftelsesregisteret — this is the general foundation registry and does not itself confer charitable status. Both commercial and charitable foundations are listed here.

Microsoft validates Norwegian nonprofit status through its partner Percent.org / TechSoup, which checks the Frivillighetsregisteret and sector classification. BISS Gausel would not pass this validation.

📄 Microsoft's Nonprofit Eligibility Requirement

Microsoft requires organizations to be:
"A nonprofit or non-governmental organization with recognition of nonprofit status from the relevant government authority, equivalent to 501(c)(3) status under the United States Internal Revenue Code."

In Norway, the equivalent recognized form is either registration in Frivillighetsregisteret or formal recognition as an ideell (charitable/non-commercial) organization by Skatteetaten. None of the three BISS entities meet this criterion.

🔗 Source: microsoft.com/en-us/nonprofits/eligibility

🎓 What BISS Can Use Instead — Microsoft 365 Education

Good news: BISS likely qualifies for Microsoft 365 Education licensing

Schools that are accredited educational institutions — regardless of for-profit or nonprofit status — can qualify for Microsoft 365 Education through Microsoft's Education program. This is separate from the nonprofit program:

  • Microsoft 365 A1 — Free for students and teachers at eligible schools
  • Microsoft 365 A3/A5 — Significantly discounted for accredited schools

The Azure costs in this document use standard Pay-As-You-Go pricing which applies to BISS. Azure for Education credits are typically only for students/research, not for school administrative infrastructure.
