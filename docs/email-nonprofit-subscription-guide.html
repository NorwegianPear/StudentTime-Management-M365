How to Order an Azure Subscription (Nonprofit Model)

Hi Adnan,

To deploy the Student Access Management solution, BISS needs an Azure subscription. As a nonprofit organization, BISS qualifies for Microsoft’s nonprofit program which includes a free $2,000 USD Azure credit per year — no credit card required.

Below are the steps to register, and what documentation you need to provide.

📝 Documentation Required

Have the following ready before you start (the application takes about 10 minutes):

# Document / Information Details
1 Organization registration number Your Norwegian “Organisasjonsnummer” from Brønnøysundregistrene. This is the legal identifier Microsoft uses to verify your nonprofit status.
2 Official organization name The legal name as registered — e.g., “Bergan International School Stavanger” (must match the Brønnøysund registration)
3 Physical address The official registered address for BISS
4 Contact person details Name, email address, and phone number of the person submitting the application
5 Microsoft 365 Global Admin account You must sign in with a Global Admin account to apply (e.g., your admin@biss.no or @biss.onmicrosoft.com account)

💡 If BISS already has Microsoft 365 Education licenses, you may already be registered as a nonprofit. Sign into nonprofit.microsoft.com with your admin account to check. If so, skip straight to Step 2 below.

🚀 Step-by-Step: Ordering the Subscription

Step 1: Register as a Microsoft Nonprofit

  1. Go to https://nonprofit.microsoft.com/en-us/getting-started
  2. Click “Eligibility pre-check” first to confirm BISS qualifies (international schools are typically eligible)
  3. Then click “Apply” under “Apply for nonprofit grants and discounts”
  4. Sign in with your Global Admin account
  5. Fill in: organization name, country (Norway), Organisasjonsnummer, address, and contact info
  6. Submit — Microsoft reviews within 1–5 business days and sends an approval email

Step 2: Activate the Azure Grant

After approval:

  1. Sign into https://nonprofit.microsoft.com (the Nonprofit Hub)
  2. Go to the “Azure” or “Offers” section
  3. Click “Activate” next to the Azure Grant ($2,000 USD/year)
  4. Follow the prompts — this creates a nonprofit billing account in Azure

No credit card required. The grant renews annually.

Step 3: Create the Azure Subscription

  1. Go to https://portal.azure.com
  2. Search for “Subscriptions” in the top bar → click “+ Add”
  3. Fill in:
    • Subscription name: e.g., BISS-Production
    • Billing account: the nonprofit billing account from Step 2
    • Plan: Microsoft Azure Plan
  4. Click “Review + create” → “Create”

The subscription is created immediately.

Step 4: Send Me the Details

Once the subscription is created, please reply to this email with:

Subscription ID Azure Portal → Subscriptions → click your subscription → Overview → copy the “Subscription ID”
Tenant ID Entra Admin Center (entra.microsoft.com) → Overview → “Tenant ID”
