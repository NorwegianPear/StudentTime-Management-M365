Hi Adnan,

You asked about paying annually vs monthly for Azure — here is a clear breakdown of all commitment options and exactly what BISS would pay under each. The key cost driver is the App Service B1 (the webserver that runs the management portal). Azure Automation itself is essentially free regardless of commitment model.

💡 Script-Only option has no reservation decisions to make. Azure Automation and Log Analytics are consumption-based services — there are no reserved instances or annual commitments available for these at BISS's usage scale. Cost remains ~25–55 NOK/month on Pay-As-You-Go regardless. If BISS chooses Script-Only, skip directly to the recommendation at the bottom.

☁️ The Only Cost Subject to Commitment Discounts: App Service B1

The portal is hosted on an Azure App Service Plan B1 in Norway East. This is the one resource where Microsoft offers significant discounts for annual or 3-year commitments via Reserved Instances.

App Service B1 spec: 1 vCore, 1.75 GB RAM, Windows. Norway East region.
Azure Automation + Log Analytics: Billed on consumption — no reservation model applies at this scale. ~25–55 NOK/month regardless of commitment choice.

🌟 Recommendation for BISS

📄 If BISS chooses Script-Only (no portal)

→ Pay-As-You-Go. No decision needed.
Azure Automation and Log Analytics have no reserved instance options at this scale. Monthly cost is ~25–55 NOK regardless. No commitment, no risk.
🌐 If BISS adds the portal — first 12 months

→ Start on Pay-As-You-Go.
For a new deployment, Pay-As-You-Go gives you full flexibility. If something needs to change (region, tier, solution direction) there is no financial penalty. The extra cost vs 1-year reserved is ~252 NOK/month — a small premium for full flexibility during the initial period.
🌐 If BISS adds the portal — after first year of stable use

→ 1-Year Reserved, monthly billing (no upfront).
Once the solution is proven and stable, switching to a 1-year reserved (monthly billing, no upfront) saves ~3,024 NOK/year (~35% off) with zero upfront cash risk. If the school's situation changes, the reservation can be exchanged for a different Azure resource. This is the best balance of savings and flexibility.
⚠️ 3-Year Reserved — only if BISS is very confident long-term

The 3-year option saves an additional ~864–1,728 NOK/year over the 1-year option, but locks BISS in for 36 months with very limited exit options. Given that BISS's Azure spend for this solution is relatively low (~9,000 NOK/year with portal), the financial upside of going 3-year vs 1-year is modest (~72–144 NOK/month extra saving) while the commitment risk is significant. Not recommended for a first Azure deployment.
📈 Break-even note for 1-year all-upfront vs monthly:
Paying the 1-year reserved all upfront (~5,328 NOK) saves ~288 NOK vs the monthly committed billing (~5,616 NOK/year) — that's ~24 NOK/month savings for paying in one lump sum. For most schools, the cash-flow benefit of monthly billing outweighs this small difference. 1-year reserved monthly billing is the practical sweet spot.
